## ProcessesV1

### Available Operations

* [GetProcessInfo](/resources/processesv1/get_process_info) - Get details for a [process](https://hathora.dev/docs/concepts/hathora-entities#process).
* [GetRunningProcesses](/resources/processesv1/get_running_processes) - Retrieve 10 most recently started [process](https://hathora.dev/docs/concepts/hathora-entities#process) objects for an [application](https://hathora.dev/docs/concepts/hathora-entities#application). Filter the array by optionally passing in a `region`.
* [GetStoppedProcesses](/resources/processesv1/get_stopped_processes) - Retrieve 10 most recently stopped [process](https://hathora.dev/docs/concepts/hathora-entities#process) objects for an [application](https://hathora.dev/docs/concepts/hathora-entities#application). Filter the array by optionally passing in a `region`.
